Back on the Wagon

April 10, 2011

School’s out, probably for summer, and I’ve been inspired to blog again by the recent flurry of posts on self-publishing. But when I logged in after all these months since NaNoWriMo, Drupal distracted me from my intended topic by telling me I’ve been a member of my website for over six years. It’s been about that long since I sold my first story.

So I had to go dig up my first post about Bloxsom and my other first post about converting my blog from Bloxsom to Drupal (and from a linkblog to a real blog). That, of course, reminded me how I really need to upgrade Drupal, and what a nightmare that always is. I also need to update SFFMS and, you know, write.

Making a list is the first step…
