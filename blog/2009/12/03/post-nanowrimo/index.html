Post-NaNoWriMo

December 3, 2009

I took December 1st off, but according to Alexandra Sokoloff, I don’t deserve a break from my novel until it’s actually finished. I repented yesterday and went back to my novel, and also redeemed my 50% off coupon for Scrivener.

I’m not sure I’ll be doing any of the other things suggested on my I Wrote a Novel, Now What? page. The free proof of my novel from CreateSpace and NaNoFiMo sound tempting, but I have other things to write in December besides the second half of my novel.
