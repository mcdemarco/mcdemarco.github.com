The Small Zombie Apocalypse Press

July 27, 2008

Via a contributor and fellow Odfellow: Bits of the Dead is a zombie flash fiction anthology out from Coscom Entertainment.

In my search for the publisher I came across another small zombie-apocalypse press, Permuted Press, with an anthology currently open for August: The World is Dead, paying a penny a word.

I also found some exhaustive lists of new zombie fiction and reviews at All Things Zombie, which led me to last year’s Monster Planet [available free online or from Amazon], the third volume in a zombie apocalypse series I’ve been reading. Apparently Publishers Weekly has declared 2008 the Year of the Zombie, so I’m not as late to the brains party as I’d thought.
