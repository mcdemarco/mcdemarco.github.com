Banana Cranberry Muffins

This one is from allrecipes, also cut down to about 9 muffins.

Ingredients

1 c. fresh or frozen cranberries ½ c. sugar 1 c. water

1/3 c. sugar 1/6 c. shortening 1 eggs 7/8 c. all-purpose flour 1 tsp baking powder ¼ tsp salt 1/8 tsp baking soda ½ c. mashed ripe banana ¼ c. chopped walnuts

Directions

Boil cranberries in water with ½ c. sugar. Simmer uncovered 5-7 minutes or until berries start to pop. Drain and set aside.

Cream shortening and remaining sugar. Add egg and beat. Combine the flour, baking powder, salt and baking soda; add to mixture along with banana.

Fold in cranberries and nuts.

Bake muffins at 400Â° for 15-20 minutes or until the toothpick test. Cool in pan 5 minutes then on wire racks.
